File-Based Password Management for Privacy Enthusiasts

PassManage is an open-source, free password manager that stores your passwords locally, providing you with a stress-free alternative to cloud-based password storage solutions. It uses local storage, so you don't have to worry about your passwords being exposed to third parties or being lost in the cloud.
